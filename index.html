Hi, my name is Mara and I currently reside in Orlando, FL. I am enrolled in the University of Central Florida’s Coding Bootcamp, where I am learning about HTML, CSS, and Javascript concepts. It’s a bit of a leap from creative writing to coding, but I believe where there is risk, there’s an opportunity to learn and grow.

No matter what role or industry I find myself in, I strive to be a thoughtful subject matter expert and a trusted customer advocate in order to overcome blockers, enhance the customer experience and drive innovation.

When I am not working or studing, I love reading David Sedaris, trying out new recipes, and daydreaming of eating tacos in Coppenhagen once the pandemic is over.
